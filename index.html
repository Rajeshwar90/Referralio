REFERRALIO – How it works ?

Referralio is a SMART PHONE based solution for doctors and institutions, to manage referral network. This brings together referring doctor, Hospital Doctor, patients, Hospital management and corporate clients on a single platform.

1

Helps Hospitals / Institutions

Helps Hospitals / Institutions create and manage a “SMART” private referral System.

2

Define Doctors

With this mobile application, Hospital can define Hospital doctors and referring doctors.

3

Refer Patients

Once this is set, referring doctors can refer patients to corporate doctors through the app and the hospital doctor can instantly respond to the referring doctor, on care procedures, follow ups etc.

4

Requests & Respond

Hospital will also be in the know how of such requests and can respond to the doctors and patients accordingly.

5

Referral Loop.

Completes referral loop.

6

Push Notifications

Hospital can communicate with their entire referral network with one single click through push notifications. It could be about New research paper, CEOs message, new PET scan, Onboarding of specialist etc.

Problem that we are trying to Solve

Open Referral Loop

Referring doctors don’t hear back from Hospital doctors for continued care of patients. Hence referral loop doesn’t get closed. As per a survey, un-responded rate is 70% at teaching Hospitals and 40% at corporate Hospital set ups.

Paper based

75% of Hospital doctors opined that responding to referring doctor is a must considering continued care of the patient. However they don’t find time to respond, since this process currently is paper based.

Awareness

70% of referring doctors opined that they come to know about Hospital updates only through word of mouth. Hence there is a need for a communication platform to know about say, a new PET scan, onboarded superspecialty

How are we attempting to solve this problem ?

Institutionalises Referral network

Hospitals can manage referred patients with superior care and follow up instructions for continued care.

Streamlined WorkFlow with a personal touch

Doctors can still interact through the app and SMS with each other, however Hospital would be part of this communication and provide superior care to patients.

Its instant, efficient & paperless

Since it is SMART PHONE based, referrals can be sent and response can be received instantly.

Top of mind

It enables “top of mind” for Hospitals with Referring doctors, builds a brand, fosters communication, by way of updates about new facilities, new treatment protocols, seminars instantly.
